About Onstra

We got tired of watching service businesses duct tape generic software together. So we started building what they actually needed.

Our Story

Onstra started from frustration. We watched talented service business owners waste hours on tools that almost fit, lose jobs to disorganization, and burn out managing operations that existed entirely in their heads.

We knew the answer was not another SaaS subscription. It was software built from scratch around how each business actually operates.

What We Believe

Your time is too valuable to spend fighting software that was not built for you. Every business deserves a system that fits how it works, not the other way around.

We believe in solutions that are practical and built to last. No fluff, no upsells, no contracts that lock you in. Just software that solves real problems.

How We Work

We sit down with you, understand your operation, identify what is broken, and build the right thing. Whether that is a full CRM, a field service app, a job management PWA, or workflow automation, we scope it right and build it fast.

We move quickly, stay communicative, and obsess over making your systems work. Because when your business runs better, we have done our job.
