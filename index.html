Onstra was born from frustration. We watched talented service business owners waste hours on repetitive tasks, lose leads to disorganization, and burn out trying to scale operations that ran entirely from memory.

We knew there had to be a better way. Not through generic advice or cookie-cutter solutions, but through real automation that actually works for how service businesses operate.
